What I Made:

I made a webpage that shows my support and understanding on the Philadelphia Phillies franchise. I am a huge fan and they are my favorite sports team.

Screenshot or Visual:

Project Link:

My Phillies Fan Project

What This Was Supposed to Communicate:

This was made to show my knowledge and appreciation for the Phillies organization.

Who My Audience Was:

Sports fans, but specifically Philly Sports fans. The people who are similar in knowledge to me.

One Design Choice I Made on Purpose:

I made the background of the header 2 kids playing baseball, I thought this really fit the theme I had going. I also included the colors of the Phillies, which are red, white, & blue.

Why That Design Choice Helped the Audience:

The designs I included helps the audience understand the theme of the website. People see red, white, and blue, and also baseball and immediately think of the Philadelphia Phillies. I also included a heading that says "Ring the Bell" showing support and recognization of the city of Philadelphia.

One Thing I Would Improve:

One thing that I would change is the fact that the website is pretty limited, it has some cool and interesting information about the team, but it could have much more. Also, I added a "Schedule" button in the top right, but did not include an image or list of teams they play this year.
