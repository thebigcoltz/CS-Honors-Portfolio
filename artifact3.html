What This Project Was:

This project was groups of data being brought in, and then the data built my app that predicts or recommends what a person's favorite fast food restaurant is, or should be.

The Problem or Question:

The problem I was trying to solve is that people may not have a favorite fast food restaurant, and this app was made to give a suggestion or an answer.

The Data, Features, or Labels Involved:

The features are the biggest part of this, the feature that are in my app are people's personal preferences when it comes to fast food. The labels are also important too. The data is what really goes in to the app and helps it function though.

What the AI System Was Supposed to Do:

The system is supposed to generate a suggestion or a predicition on a person's favorite fast food restaurant.

One Limitation, Bias Risk, or Ethical Concern:

A limitation to my data was that we only had certain, limited options for each question we asked. We also included only 5 restaurants that me and my partner agreed on, when we know there is lots more we could add into this app.

One Way a Human Decision Mattered:

A decision I made was to keep adding factors until the AI's accuracy was at a % that was good enough for this app.

One Way This Project Could Be Improved:

I feel like there is alot I could improve. The most obvious improvement to make would to be to add more options for each question so that our data is a wider variety. Also, all of the people who responded eat food that come from animals, which tons of people do not morally agree with, and this would need to be changed for this app to be accepted and used truthfully and accurately.
