Technology Impact and Responsibility:

The Issue:

Data Breaches and Cyber-Threats.

Who Is Affected:

A data breach is affecting anyone whose personal information is held somewhere online, specifically in mainstream datacenters.

One Benefit:

If a company or an organization goes through a data breach, they are usually much more prepared for the future.

One Risk

If your data is leaked, you may experience identity theft, fraud, or phishing directly aimed at you.

One Tradeoff:

A major tradeoff is the balance between security and the system usage.

One Responsibility Creators or Users Have

Make sure your data is not TOO public, some people live there entire lives online, and that puts you and a very big risk.
