The Internet, Cloud, Cybersecurity, or Privacy Concept Explained:

My artifact explains and tells you how to detect and act against phishing emails or messages.

The Risk or Threat Involved:

People could still fall for phishing emails or messages if they are forgetful or clumsy, and thats just me being honest. If you read all of my information and still fall for phishing, then thats on you.

At Least Two Defenses:

  1. Defense 1: Hover Method - When you hover over a link in an email, it shows you where the link may take you, this is a good way to spot scam.
  2. Defense 2: Report Phishing - In most emails, there will be a report scam button where you can report the sender of an email.

Why Human Choices Matter:

Humans decisions are the biggest part of this, if they cannot make the right decision or spot the scam, then they will most likely fall victimm to it.

Which Defense Would Matter Most, and Why:

I feel like all of the defenses are important, but making sure that your browsing habits are safe, and that you have password managers to stay secure in a smart way.
